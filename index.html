about us

A Brilliant Invention Creation of the Decent Mind

We are a non-profit research organization, whose activities are focused on advancing the understanding and application of health security and behavioural science principles to protect communities and individuals from emerging health risks..

Through research, training/mentorship, and collaboration, we strive to develop innovative strategies, interventions and partnerships that promote behavioural change, strengthen public health infrastructure, and enhance preparedness for health threats and emergencies.

To create a safer and healthier world through the integration of health security and behavioral sciences, fostering resilience and preparedness against global health threats.

Our mission is to empower individuals, communities, and organization to take proactive measures to safeguard public health, reduce vulnerability, and promote sustainable health practices. Together, we can build a resilient world where health security and behavioural science intersect to protect and improve lives.
